Privacy Policy — hexlight

Last updated: 11 June 2026

hexlight (“the app”, package com.hexlight.app) is a tool for planning hexagonal LED lighting layouts. This policy explains how it handles your information. In short: it does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data we collect

None. We have no account system, no servers that receive your data, and no analytics or tracking. We do not collect names, emails, location, contacts, advertising IDs, or usage data.

Data stored on your device

  • Your designs exist only in the app while you use it, and inside any share link or PDF you choose to create.
  • Your theme preference (light/dark) is saved in local storage on your device.

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share or export it. Uninstalling the app removes it.

Sharing & export (only when you choose)

  • Share link / QR code: the entire design is encoded into a link pointing to 147hex.pages.dev. The link is created on your device; we do not store it. Anyone you give the link to can open the design.
  • PDF export: generated on your device and handed to the Android share sheet for you to save or send.

Third-party services

  • Google Fonts: the app loads a font stylesheet from Google’s servers, which may receive your IP address as part of that request, per Google’s Privacy Policy. No other data is sent.

Permissions

The app requests only INTERNET (to load fonts and open shared links). It does not access your camera, location, files, or contacts.

Children

The app is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will change.

Contact

Questions: ualiamer@riseup.net
